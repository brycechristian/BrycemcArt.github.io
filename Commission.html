Commissions

I absolutely love doing commissions for people, especially when it means something to them! I am able to do commissions in any medium, all vary in price. 

Canvas: (Paintings)

Every square inch costs 0.50 cents. Depending on the subject matter, the price may increase or decrease. For example, asking for a portrait would increase the price whereas a painting of an animal would be lower. 

Paper: (color pencil, water color)

Every square inch costs 0.25 cents. Depending on the subject matter, the price may vary.
