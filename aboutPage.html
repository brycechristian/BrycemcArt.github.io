About Me

My name is Bryce Christian and I’ve been drawing since I was in elementary school. I’m interested in digital art, painting, and paper and pencil! In the past I have created digital portraits, paintings, charcoal, and marker pieces. Popular commissions subjects include family members, pets, and roommate portraits. Hope you enjoy my work and don’t hesitate to reach out! Find more information about the commission process in the commission page section.
